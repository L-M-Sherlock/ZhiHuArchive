难点卡片(Leech)

Leeches in SuperMemo are questions that a student keeps forgetting. Most often leeches come from disrespect for the20 rules of knowledge formulation. Some well-formulated material may also need a boost from mnemonic techniques or a boost incoherence, e.g. by setting questions in relevant context.

SuperMemo 中的 Leech 指的是学生经常遗忘的问题。大多数情况下，Leech 来自对知识表述的 20 条规则[1]的不尊重。一些表述良好的材料可能还需要加强助记技巧或增强连贯性[2]，例如在相关的上下文设置问题。
