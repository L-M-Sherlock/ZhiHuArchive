自问自答一波（嫌写文章小题大做了233

举个例子[1]

在 Cloze 的 Text 字段里输入：

Mary has been {{c1::employed in::忙于，除了用busy}} preparation for the trip all afternoon.

挖空效果：

Mary has been [忙于，除了用busy] preparation for the trip all afternoon.

查看答案:

Mary has been employed in preparation for the trip all afternoon.
